about us

Alameda Science and Technology Intsitute (ASTI) Robotics Team, #4186 was established in 2011 with a membership of only ten students and two dedicated mentors. Mentor team MVRT (115) aided the Aztechs in fielding an operational robot at the 2012 Silicon Valley Regional, and the team has been developing ever more advanced robots each year. That growing expertise got the team into the semi-finals of the 2014 CalGames Invitational, and with a team full of new faces and returning veterans we are ready to build an even better robot next year!
